Frequently Asked Questions

What is Twofer?

Twofer is a live local specials app for customers and an AI-assisted promotion tool for local businesses.

Are the specials real?

Yes. The two cards on this page are illustrated samples; open the app to see live specials near you.

Why do businesses need to be approved?

New business accounts are reviewed to keep published specials tied to real local businesses.

What kinds of promotions can businesses create?

Businesses can create 2-for-1, free-item, and deep-discount offers with controlled time windows and quantities.

Is Twofer free for customers?

Yes. Twofer is free for customers.

Where is Twofer available?

Twofer is live in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, with more cities planned.
