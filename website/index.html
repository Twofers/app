Frequently Asked Questions

What is Twofer?

Twofer is a live local specials app for customers and an AI-assisted promotion tool for local businesses.

Are the specials real right now?

The current public preview uses sample offers while the DFW pilot opens to approved testers and businesses.

Why do businesses need an access code?

Business tools are reviewed during testing so published specials stay tied to approved local businesses.

What kinds of promotions can businesses create?

Businesses can create 2-for-1, free-item, and deep-discount offers with controlled time windows and quantities.

Is Twofer free for customers?

Yes. Twofer is free for customers during the testing period.

Where is Twofer testing first?

Twofer is previewing first in the Dallas-Fort Worth area.
