Support

For help with Twofer, email support@twoferapp.com.

Account deletion

You can start account deletion from inside the Twofer app under Account or Settings. You can also contact support if you need help, or use the delete account page.

Business support

Businesses can request access on the business page. Business accounts are reviewed before activation. Billing questions are handled through Twofer support and approved web/admin billing flows.

Report an offer

In the app, open the offer detail screen and choose Report this offer. You can also email support with the business name, offer title, and what happened.

Privacy contact

For privacy or data deletion questions, email support@twoferapp.com.
