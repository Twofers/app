Business Terms

Business account access is reviewed before activation.

Business responsibility

Businesses are responsible for accurate profiles, offer content, schedules, quantities, eligibility terms, customer service, staff access, and honoring valid published offers according to displayed terms.

AI-assisted copy

AI-assisted offer copy is a drafting tool. Businesses must review all copy, images, terms, and localized text before publishing and must not publish misleading or unsafe offers.

Prohibited offers

Launch offers may not promote alcohol, tobacco or nicotine, cannabis or drugs, weapons, adult sexual services or products, prescription medication, illegal products or services, counterfeit goods, discriminatory offers, or misleading bait-and-switch offers.

Community conduct

Offers, images, and profile content must not be objectionable, offensive, or abusive. Customers can report a business or offer from inside the app, and can hide a business from their feed. We review reports and act on them within 24 hours, including removing offers and suspending accounts that break these rules.

Billing and cancellation

Approved business billing is handled through reviewed web/admin flows and Stripe where applicable. The mobile app does not provide public subscription purchase, checkout, pricing links, Apple Pay, or Google Pay for launch.

Promotional Materials

The Business may authorize Twofer to provide and place removable promotional materials, including countertop displays, window decals, QR-code signs, or similar materials, at participating locations. The Business retains control over the location and display of all materials and may move or remove them at any time. Twofer will not permanently attach promotional materials or intentionally damage the premises. The Business represents that it has the authority to approve the placement of promotional materials or has obtained any required permission from the property owner, landlord, franchisor, or other applicable party.

This authorization is optional and is granted separately; accepting these Business Terms does not itself authorize placement of promotional materials.

Removal and suspension

Twofer may remove offers, limit publishing, or suspend business accounts to protect customers, enforce policy, prevent abuse, or comply with law.
