Delete Account

You can request deletion of your Twofer account from inside the app or by contacting support.

Delete from the app

Sign in to Twofer, open Account or Settings, and choose the account deletion option. The app will guide you through confirmation before deletion starts.

Request help by email

Email support@twoferapp.com from the email address on your Twofer account and include "Delete my Twofer account" in the subject line.

What may be retained

Some security, transaction, fraud-prevention, business, support, or legal records may be retained when required or anonymized where appropriate. Twofer does not sell personal data.
