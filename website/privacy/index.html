Privacy Policy

This policy describes the data used by the current Twofer app and website launch flow.

Data we collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, login identifiers, and role.
  • Consumer profile details such as ZIP code, birthday, favorites, claims, redemptions, and preferences.
  • Location data when you choose GPS-based nearby discovery, plus ZIP fallback preferences.
  • Push notification tokens and alert settings when you opt in.
  • Business details such as business name, address, contact person, email, phone, offer content, offer images, metrics, and staff access details where applicable.
  • Business access request details submitted on the website.
  • Usage, diagnostics, support messages, and app analytics needed to operate and improve Twofer.

How we use data

We use data to provide account access, nearby offer discovery, claims, QR redemption, merchant tools, AI-assisted offer creation, support, abuse prevention, analytics, notifications, and legal compliance.

AI-assisted tools

Business AI tools help draft offer copy and related creative materials. Businesses must review AI-assisted content before publishing. Voice input for AI-assisted composition is processed ephemerally for transcription and drafting; Twofer does not store raw voice audio, though transcript, usage, and support metadata may be retained to operate the service.

Service providers

Twofer uses service providers such as Supabase for backend services, Stripe for approved web/admin business billing, push notification providers, map/location services, AI providers, diagnostics tools, and email/support providers where configured.

No sale or tracking

Twofer does not sell personal data. Twofer does not use personal data for cross-company tracking for this launch posture. Business billing is handled through reviewed web/admin flows where applicable, not mobile subscription purchase, mobile checkout, Apple Pay, or Google Pay in the app.

Account deletion

You can start account deletion inside the app under Account or Settings. You can also review the delete account page.
