About Penguin Falls

Penguin Falls LLC is a Texas-based digital product company founded by a retired U.S. Coast Guard veteran and SMU Cox MBA graduate. The company focuses on practical software products that help local businesses connect with customers, improve operations, and create measurable value.

Penguin Falls was created to own, operate, and develop digital products, beginning with Twofer.

Our First Product

Twofer

Twofer is a real-time demand activation platform for local businesses. It helps coffee shops, cafés, bakeries, and other local merchants create controlled, limited-time BOGO offers designed to bring customers in during slower periods.

Twofer focuses on simple offer creation, live local discovery, and frictionless QR-based redemption.
