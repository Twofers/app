SHORT DESCRIPTION (≤80 chars typical)
Local 2-for-1 style deals: browse, claim, show QR at checkout.

FULL DESCRIPTION (outline)
- Discover active deals from nearby businesses.
- Save favorites and get optional alerts when they post deals.
- Claim deals to your in-app wallet; redeem with a QR code at the business.
- Business accounts: create a profile, optional location text and coordinates for “Near me” distance sorting, post deals with posters and schedules.

LOCATION (why we need it)
Used only when you tap “Near me”: we read approximate device location to sort deals by distance when businesses have set latitude/longitude, or to match your city/region to the free-text Location field on business profiles. We do not sell location data.

CAMERA
Used to scan QR codes when redeeming a claimed deal at a business.

NOTIFICATIONS (optional)
If you enable deal alerts, we send local/push-style notifications about new deals from businesses you favorited. You can turn this off in Account.

DATA & ACCOUNTS
Sign-in and deal data are handled through the app backend (Supabase). See your privacy policy URL for retention, deletion, and contact.
