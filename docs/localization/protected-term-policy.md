## Protected By Default

The following stay unchanged unless the merchant supplies an approved localized name:

- Business names
- Branded product names
- Trademarked menu items
- Merchant-provided do-not-translate terms
- Claim codes, QR payloads, redemption tokens, and technical identifiers

## Approved Localized Names

An approved localized name must record:

- Original term
- Locale
- Approved display name
- Approver
- Date
- Version

Live deals snapshot the exact term versions used. Later dictionary edits must not silently change an already approved or published ad.

## AI Suggestions

AI may suggest localized terms during internal workflows, but an unreviewed suggestion cannot become a verified catalog term automatically. Unknown or branded terms must be preserved and flagged for later native review when appropriate.
