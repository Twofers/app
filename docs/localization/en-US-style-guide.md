# Twofer en-US Style Guide

Status: internal owner recorded. Native reviewer: Dan / Twofer admin.

## Exact Offer Mechanics

- Render exact offer mechanics from structured offer facts, not from translated prose.
- Use clear customer-action language: "Buy one latte and get one free", "Buy a bagel and get a free coffee", or "Get 40% off one cold brew".
- Do not use shorthand such as "BOGO", "2-for-1", "1+1", or unexplained promo codes.
- Keep business names, branded product names, and merchant-supplied protected terms unchanged unless the merchant supplies an approved localized name.

## Tone

- Prefer simple local-business language over hype.
- Avoid unsupported claims such as "best", "healthiest", "guaranteed", or "exclusive" unless those facts are provided and verified.
- Keep the exact offer line short enough for mobile cards.

## Reviewer Notes

- English templates are the internal baseline for structured mechanics.
- Any new template family must be recorded in the native review log before broad production rollout.
